Where to Begin

If you are just starting out in Computer Security / Cyber Security it can be very difficult and imtimidating. This is why we have created a short guide of tips and resources to help get you started.

Education Nights: These talks can offer a great amount of help to anyone just starting out, and any questions you have can and will be answered.

TryHackMe: THM is a learning platform focused on breaking down complex tools and strategies used in the Cyber Secuirty world and teaching them through interactive tools and machines. (TryHackMe)

Competitions: Even if you dont think you are skilled enough to compete in anything you still should. You have nothing to lose and everything to gain from it.
