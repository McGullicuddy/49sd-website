How to Join

In order to join the club simply send us an email at (49thSecurityDivision@uncc.edu) and then join us on slack.

Doing so will allow you to:

  • Attend meetings
  • Attend competition practices(For competitions that do not require membership)
  • Attend lab hours (whenever we have them -- not this semester, of course)



Becoming a Member

In order to become a member of the 49th you must first contribute to the club.

Some ways you can contribute to the club are:

  • Commit to our GitHub
  • Submit talks for education nights and competition practices
  • Submit projects you are working on (Slack or GitHub)
  • Attend Competition Practices and General Meetings
  • Help in anyway to improve the club

Doing so will allow you to:

  • Hold Officer positions
    • Looks good on a resume
    • Is actually cool
  • Access to being on the UNCC competition team
  • Ability to vote and make changes to the club
  • Potential lab access and access to our infrastructure, internal projects
