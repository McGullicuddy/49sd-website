About the 49th

The 49th Security Division is a Computer Security and Ethical Hacking club. The club welcomes anyone of any skill set that is interested in computer security and looking to further their education in this field. We compete in competitions such as CyberForce, CCDC, and NCL. We also offer weekly education nights and competition practices.
